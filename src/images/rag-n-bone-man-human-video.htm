Since releasing his single 'Human', Rory Graham, aka Rag 'N' Bone Man has been creating quite a name for himself. Though Rory's been releasing music for the past year or so, 2016 has really been the year he's managed to breakthrough. Having performed at a number of festivals over the summer, over the recent months, Rory has also been seen supporting Tom Odell on his UK tour.
